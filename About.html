About Us

Welcome to Pallo, where every dish tells a story of Italian tradition and culinary excellence. Pallo is a cozy yet vibrant dining destination that invites you to experience the warmth and flavors of Italy right at your table.

About Pallo

Established in 1994, Pallo has been a cornerstone of Rome culinary scene, bringing the authentic flavors of Italy to our cherished patrons for over 30 years. Since our inception, we've remained dedicated to upholding the rich traditions of Italian cuisine while infusing it with our own unique flair and passion for excellence.
